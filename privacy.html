1. Overview

GloSkin ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the GloSkin mobile application (the "App") and related services.

By downloading or using GloSkin, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

We will never sell your personal data to third parties. Your skin photos and health-related information are used solely to provide you with personalized skincare guidance within the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: Email address and password (or Apple Sign-In / Google Sign-In credentials) when you create an account.
  • Skin profile data: Skin type, skin concerns, sensitivities, allergies, and skincare goals collected during onboarding and updated over time.
  • Skin diary entries: Notes, journal text, and mood or skin-condition logs you choose to record.
  • Product data: Products you add to your routines, scan via the ingredient scanner, or mark as favorites.
  • AI chat messages: Questions and conversations you have with the Skincare Guru AI assistant.

2.2 Photos and Camera Data

  • Face scan photos: When you perform a skin analysis scan, your camera captures images of your face. These images are processed to generate your Glowi Score and skin health assessment.
  • Check-in selfies: Photos you take for your daily progress timeline are stored securely in your account.
  • Product ingredient photos: Images captured when using the product scanner feature to analyze ingredient labels.

Camera access is requested only when you initiate a scan or photo feature. You may grant or revoke camera access at any time in your device's Settings.

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage data: Features used, screens visited, scan frequency, routine check-off rates, and session duration.
  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and app version.
  • Crash reports and diagnostics: Error logs, crash data, and performance metrics to help us improve stability.

2.4 Purchase Information

Subscription purchases (GloSkin Pro) are processed entirely through Apple's in-app purchase system. We do not receive or store your payment card information. We receive only a transaction receipt and subscription status from Apple and/or RevenueCat (our subscription management provider).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App and its features.
  • Generate your personalized Glowi Score and skin health assessments.
  • Create and update your customized skincare routines and product recommendations.
  • Power the Skincare Guru AI chat with context about your skin profile.
  • Build and display your progress selfie timeline and skin diary.
  • Send push notifications you have opted into (routine reminders, scan prompts).
  • Manage your subscription and verify Pro access.
  • Diagnose technical problems and improve app performance.
  • Comply with legal obligations.
We do not use your face scan photos, skin profile, or health-related data for advertising targeting or to build third-party advertising profiles.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We share data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating the App. These providers are contractually bound to use your data only for the purposes we specify and to maintain appropriate security standards. Current providers include:

  • Supabase: Cloud database and authentication infrastructure (data storage, user accounts).
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and in-app purchase validation.
  • Sentry: Crash reporting and error monitoring.
  • AI Vision API providers (e.g., OpenAI or Anthropic): Face scan images and chat messages are processed by AI APIs to generate assessments and responses. These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

4.3 Business Transfers

If GloSkin is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will provide notice before your data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4.4 With Your Consent

We may share your information for any other purpose with your explicit consent.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase's cloud infrastructure, which uses industry-standard encryption both at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+).

Face scan photos and check-in selfies are stored in your private, access-controlled account storage and are not publicly accessible.

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at support@gloskin.app.

6. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy governing how it handles data:

Supabase

Provides backend database, authentication, and file storage. Supabase stores your account data, skin profile, diary entries, and photos.

RevenueCat

Manages in-app subscription purchases and entitlements. RevenueCat receives your App Store transaction data and subscription status to validate your GloSkin Pro access.

Sentry

Collects crash reports and error diagnostics. Sentry may receive device information and anonymized stack traces when the App experiences an error. No personal identifiable health or photo data is sent to Sentry.

Apple Sign-In / Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in using Apple or Google, these services authenticate your identity and provide a unique user identifier. We receive only the information you authorize during sign-in.

Apple App Store

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. Apple's privacy policy governs the collection of payment and purchase data.

Affiliate Partners

Product recommendation links may include affiliate tracking parameters (e.g., Amazon Associates). Clicking these links may allow affiliate partners to set cookies and track purchases. We do not share your personal profile with affiliate partners — the links are generic and not personalized on the third-party side.

7. Your Rights & Choices

Access and Correction

You may access and update your skin profile, personal information, and account details at any time within the App's Settings screen.

Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data. To delete your account:

  • Open GloSkin → Settings → Account → Delete Account, or
  • Email us at support@gloskin.app with the subject "Delete My Account."

We will permanently delete your account, skin profile, photos, diary entries, and usage history within 30 days of your request. Note that deletion is irreversible.

Data Export

You may request a copy of your personal data by emailing support@gloskin.app with the subject "Data Export Request." We will provide your data in a portable format within 30 days.

Push Notifications

You can opt out of push notifications at any time via your device's Settings or within the App under Settings → Notifications.

Camera Access

You can revoke camera access at any time via your device's Settings → Privacy → Camera. Revoking camera access will disable scan features.

Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your GloSkin Pro subscription at any time through the App Store. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. See Terms of Service for details.

California and GDPR Rights

If you are a California resident or located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you may have additional rights including the right to know, delete, portability, restriction of processing, and objection to processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@gloskin.app.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal compliance purposes (e.g., transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years for tax and accounting purposes).

Anonymized, aggregated usage statistics (which cannot be tied back to you) may be retained indefinitely to improve the App.

9. Children's Privacy

GloSkin is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete it from our systems.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has used GloSkin and provided personal data, please contact us immediately at support@gloskin.app.

Users between 13–17 should use the App only with parental consent.

10. International Users

GloSkin is operated from the United States. If you are accessing the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection rules than your country.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
  • Sending an in-app notification or push notification (for material changes).

Your continued use of GloSkin after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

  • Email: support@gloskin.app
  • Support page: gloskin.app/support

We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.
